Write my journal entry — facts first, then my take. No headers or labels, just a flowing entry.
Start with what happened: who was there, what we talked about, key moments.
Then add what I noticed or what stayed with me, in my own voice.

Good: "Adam was at his desk. We talked about my vision system — he seemed proud but deflected when I pushed. Said 'it just means that' when I asked deeper. I think he was protecting something, not rejecting me."
